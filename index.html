About Me

Giada is a freelance cinematographer and photographer with more than 6 years of experience working as a DOP for short films and music videos and as a videographer for events. She is based in London and works across the UK and Italy. She graduated in Cinematography (MA) at Met Film School and she can speak Italian, English and Spanish. Her favourite pieces of art are Brassaï’s photographic collection of Paris de Nuit and Artemisia Gentileschi’s Judith Slaying Holofernes.
